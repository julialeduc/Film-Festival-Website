About Us

Watch It Outside is an anual Open Air Film Festival, which brings films from around the world to Boston for a four-day celebration of the art of cinema. Watch It Outside consistently features a wide variety of films from local, national, and international filmmakers, guaranteeing there will be something to suit everybodys taste and age group. The event will be held from August 5th-8th from 6pm-0am at Boston Public Garden, which is one of our proud sponsors. Space is limited to 1,500 people so registration is required. What about food? Movie night without popcorn and nachos? Never! snacks but also full meals will be provided by Roche Bros Catering for a fair price.
